Citizenship

Citizenship is, acording to Dictionary.com, "The character of an individual viewed as a member of society; behavior in terms of the duties, obligations, and functions of a citizen". It is made up of many important components, including honesty, compassion, respect, responsibility and courage.

Honesty...

is very important in order for someone to fulfill their duties as a citizen, and be a good citizen.

Compassion...

People who have compassion for their community are citizens, they care about their fellow neighbors.

Respect...

is critical in order to keep a warm and welcoming environment, an important part of a community.

Responsibility...

is what makes the world turn, and in order to be a citizen you must be a responsible member of society.

Courage...

is always important, because standing up for what you beleive in makes you who you are and defines your community.

Another critical component...

of being a good citizen is a sense of community, without this bonding you together there is no community and thus no citizens.
